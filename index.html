About

I am a third-year Computer Engineering student at McMaster University and an AI/ML enthusiast with a passion for learning. My love for learning and problem-solving has allowed me to excel academically as seen through my Golden Key membership and Deans' Honour List standing.

Outside of my studies I have taken on the role of Director of Education at the McMaster Artificial Intelligence Society to share my passion. In this role I lead the Education Team of MacAI, overseeing the development and presentation of workshops on a variety of AI/ML topics and technologies. Under my leadership, we have seen a significant increase in workshop volume and attendance.

When I am not busy studying or programming I enjoy working out, cooking, and listening to music.
